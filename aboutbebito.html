About Bebito

Bebito's name mixed 2 words they are "Bebek" and "Pepito". Bebek means "baby" and petito is bear shaped candy. He is half blind but this situation not problem for he because my mom always take care of him. Therefore he is never got sick. We found him our garden in 2014. He is puffy shaped. I call him "street cat" because he always wants to go out from our house and stays out until evening. My mom always goes out and takes him to the house. He doesn't like other cats.
